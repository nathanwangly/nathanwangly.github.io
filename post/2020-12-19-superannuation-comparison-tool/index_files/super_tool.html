About this tool

What is the purpose of this calculator?

The main purpose of this calculator is to compare between different superannuation fund investment options, which may estimate different returns or have different fee structures. The calculator can also be used to compare future balances based on different contribution amounts.

The initial development of this calculator and its current structure were inspired by the Investment Frequency Calculator.

How accurate is this calculator?

To keep the calculation simple, a series of assumptions have been made that may not be perfectly accurate or may not reflect your individual circumstances. For example, the tool assumes that all contributions made will be taxed at the concessional tax rate (which is only true under your first $25,000 of contributions per financial year). It also assumes an average inflation rate of 2% p.a.

As such, you should consider the results of this tool to be illustrative and not to be precise estimates of your future balance. Please seek external advice if you are unsure of how your personal situation may differ from the assumptions made by this tool.

For those who are interested, the calculations and assumptions used for this tool are explained in more depth here.

Does this tool collect any data?

No. All calculations are performed within your browser.
