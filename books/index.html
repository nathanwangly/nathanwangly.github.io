My very rigorous rating system

How I rate the books I read:

  • ★★★★★ Books I really enjoyed and would talk to others about.
  • ★★★★☆ Books I thought were good and worth the read.
  • ★★★☆☆ Books I thought were okay but not particularly memorable.
  • ★★☆☆☆ Books I didn’t enjoy (and probably finished begrudgingly).
  • ★☆☆☆☆ Books I regretted reading.

Disclaimer: I like most books that I read, so the typical rating I give is probably 4 stars.
