My Very Rigorous Rating System

Ratings of books can be roughly translated as…

  • 5 stars: Loved it and would talk to others about it.
  • 4 stars: Thought it was pretty good.
  • 3 stars: Thought it was okay.
  • 2 stars: Didn’t really enjoy the read, but can see some merit in it.
  • 1 star: Not a good book at all. (Nothing really gets rated here though).
