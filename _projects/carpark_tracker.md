<div class="faq-container">
    <details class="faq-card">
        <summary>About the data</summary>
        <div class="faq-content">
            <p>Insights are generated by analysing real-time occupancy data made available via Transport for NSW's <a href="https://opendata.transport.nsw.gov.au/data/dataset/car-park-api">Car park API</a>.</p>
            <p>Data is provided for informational purposes only. Estimates are based on historical data and may not be up-to-date. Factors like special events, trackwork, or weather can significantly impact daily availability.</p>
        </div>
    </details>

    <details class="faq-card">
        <summary>Methodology</summary>
        <div class="faq-content">
            <p><i>Typical Range</i> is based on a 90% confidence interval. A narrow range means the number of available spots is more consistent, whereas a larger range means the number of available spots varies significantly.</p>
            <p>A car park is <i>typically full</i> by a certain time if this is observed at least 80% of the time.</p>
            <p>A car park <i>typically becomes available</i> when at least 5 spots free up (after being full). The time shown is also based on what is observed at least 80% of the time.</p>
            <p>Predictions prioritise recent behaviour over older history. A decay formula is used to allow the tool to adapt as commuting habits change over time.</p>
        </div>
    </details>
